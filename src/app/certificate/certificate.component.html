Certificates

Currently there are total of 10 Certificates in the Game. 3 for Gather, 3 for Craft and 4 for Combat.



Combat Certificates
No Certificate Name Certificate Detail
1 Warrior

Increases loot from other players, melee weapons don’t drain energy. After Promotion, frontal attacks DMG down. Meat dishes HP up. 1st attack may knock back enemies. 


Professional Skills

  • Melee Expertise: When unarmed or using non-firearms, the closer you are to an enemy, the higher your chance of blocking. Increased up to 20.
  • Breathing method: When making an unarmed strike or attacking with a non-firearms, there is a 10% chance of not using any energy. 
  • Fearless Warrior: When making an unarmed strike or attacking with a non-firearms, damage taken is decreased by 8% when standing face to face with an enemy. 
  • Carnivorous: Gain HP regeneration effect by eating meat. Different food has a different effect. 
  • Rapid Slash: When making an unarmed strike or attacking with non-firearms, the first strike has a 40% chance of making non-large enemies flee from combat. 
  • Tough Will: Grievous injury time decreases by 70% of its original amount. 
  • Critical Attack: When attacking non-large units that are no more than 5 levels above you with non-firearms  or unarmed, you have an 8% chance to inflict under attack status on the target (if it’s not the primary target, the chance is halved.) Works 1 time per 5s max. 40% of the damage you deal with be counted as Crit damage.
  • Assault Expert: When making an unarmed strike or attacking with a non-firearms, damage taken to everywhere except for your head will be reduced by 8%. When your talent reaches Level 4, non-firearm weapons slash range will increase.
  • Vigorous Posture: When holding a non-firearm, damage taken when squatting is reduced by 8%. 
  • Crippling Blow: When you deal damage with non-firearms, it has a 8% chance to make the target unable to jump and squat for the next 5 seconds. Duration increases by 0.5 seconds for every 1 attribute/ special skill upgrade the weapon has.
  • Conceal: Equip non-firearms to get close to the monster from behind and you won’t get easily discovered. Getting close to a player from behind will grant a 3% speed-up effect. After entering combat, concealment is lost.
  • Slasher: Increase the attack speed of non-firearms by 5%. Can use non-firearm attacks when jumping, firepower increases by 8% during the jump.
2 Sniper

Increases Sniper Rifle DMG. Gain extra spoils from soldiers. After Promotion, can trace enemies, and DMG to low HP enemies is increased. Headshots can Trance target. 


Professional Skills

  • Sniper Expertise: When using a sniper rifle, bow or bazooka, damage is increased by 5%.
  • Let The Bullets Fly: When using a sniper rifle, bow or bazooka, the damage is increased by 1% for every 80 yards that a bullet travels, up to a maximum of 5%.  
  • Marker Bullet: When using a sniper rifle, bow or bazooka, the enemy that is hit will be marked within 3 seconds. 
  • Tear Wound: When using a sniper rifle, bow or bazooka, when damage is dealt to the enemies with less than 40% HP, they will be injured for a continuous 5 seconds taking 3 damage per second. 
  • Head Blow: When using a sniper rifle, bow or bazooka, headshot damage is increased by 8%. The enemy receiving headshot damage gets a blurred vision for 2 seconds. 
  • Calm Blood: When gun sights are on, breath hold bar reduction speed is 10% slower. 
  • Enhanced Accessory: When using a sniper rifle, bow or bazooka is fully equipped with accessories, you’ll restore 3% HP every 10 seconds when attacking. 
  • Infectious Ammo: Damage dealt to the humanoid monster when using a sniper rifle, bow or bazooka, is increased by 8%. When attacking players, 5 health decreases every 5 seconds. After reaching Talent Level 4, heavy weapon’s damage increases.
  • Confounding Strike: When using a sniper rifle, bow or bazooka, the target cannot reload or switch weapons for the next 3 seconds after being hit with a Crit.
  • Dynamic View: When using a sniper rifle, bow or bazooka, reload speed and damage on moving targets is increased by 1%. The effect increases by 1% for every 1 attribute/ special skill upgrade the weapon has.
  • Fixed Shooting: When using a sniper rifle, you can enter crouching fire. Can’t move when using crouching fire. Increases firepower by 10%.
  • Breath Holding: The longer you hold your breath when you use a sniper rifle, the higher the damage will be. After 4 seconds, you will reach max damage of 12%. Reaching the highest value will repulse Non-Giant Infected and other players.
3 Rifleman

Increases Rifle DMG. Gain extra spoils from the Infected. After Promotion, DMG to buildings increases. Get DMG Bonuses when beating players. Med effects also increase.


Professional Skills

  • Rifle Expertise: When using an Assault Rifle, Submachine Gun, Rifile, or Shotgun, Reload Speed is increased by 8%.
  • Break The Surface: When using an assault rifle, submachine gun, rifle or shotgun to continuously deal damage to a single enemy, the damage is increased gradually up to a maximum of 5%. 
  • Destructive Madman: When using an assault rifle, submachine gun, rifle or shotgun, damage dealt with buildings is increased by 8%. 
  • Honor Badge: When using an assault rifle, submachine gun, rifle or shotgun, the damage is increased by 1% for every hostile player that is beaten down( maximum 5% ). 
  • Battlefield Recovery: When using an assault rifle, submachine gun, rifle or shotgun, medicine recovery effect is increased by 10%.
  • Crippling Fire: When using an assault rifle, submachine gun, rifle or shotgun, the first shot battle cripples the enemy, slowing them by 5% and preventing them from sprinting for the next 3 seconds. 
  • Quick Hemostasis: When using an assault rifle, submachine gun, rifle or shotgun, feebleness duration is decreased by 15%. When seriously injured, the speed of emergency treatment and medication use is increased by 15%. 
  • Drone Expert: Damage dealt with drone increased by 10%. Has a 4% chance to hit giant monsters’ weak spots. After this talent reaches Level 4, Drone skills’ CD will be reduced. 
  • Jump Suppression: When using an Assault Rifle, Submachine Gun, Rifle or Shotgun, damage dealt to enemies who are jumping is increased by 5%.  
  • Armor-piercing Missile: When using an Assault Rifle, Submachine Gun, Rifle or Shotgun to deal damage, there is a 25% chance of ignoring 1 of the enemy’s armor. 1 extra armor is ignored for every 1 attribute/ special skill upgrade the weapon has. Up to 3 Armor can be ignored. 
  • Precision: When using an Assault Rifle, Submachine Gun, Rifle or Shotgun, precision is increased by 3 when moving.
  • Drone Turbo: When using an Assault Rifle, Submachine Gun, Rifle or Shotgun, Drone gets a portion of a player’s shield according to a 15% ratio.
4 Virus

Your talent can cause the Infected to drop more Infected materials so you can make more serums. Virus damage talent attributes will be gained after upgrading.


Exclusive Items: Hardener, T-Hormone

Professional Skills

  • Sample: Additional loot can be obtained from infected in the wilderness including claw and beast tendon.
  • Activation Tech: When making serum, there is a 4% chance of obtaining an extra product.
  • Virus Bullet: Once weapon virus slots are developed, most weapons can have toxins attached. This can make your weapons deal additional virus DMG and also reduce target’s health.
  • Efficient Use: The diffuser’s effect duration for each serum is 6% longer.
  • Team Blessing: Obtained the exclusive serum formula: Hardener. The HP regeneration and shield recovery effect of the diffuser is increased by 5%.
  • Toxin Enhancement: Virus bullets provided by each bottle of toxin increased by 20%.
  • Diffusion Tech: The radius of the diffuser increases by 12%.
  • Poison Penetration: Virus bullets reduce the health of a target by 10%.
  • T-Hormone: Get the exclusive serum formula T-Hormone. Using T-Hormone in a diffuser can increase the damage bonus for teammates in the area by 10%. When using on yourself, the effect is enhanced by 12% DMG bonus.
  • Virus Effect: All virus damage increased by 3%
  • Medic’s Bane: Virus bullets have a 5% chance to cause its target to gain 10% less HP from medicine recovery for 10 seconds.
  • Viral Contagion: When dealing virus DMG to a target, there is a 1% chance of dealing 50% virus DMG to the enemies nearby.
